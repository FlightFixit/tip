Taratoor Bi Tahini (Sesame Sauce)

  • 3 cloves
    garlic
  • large pinch cayenne
  • 8 oz
    tahini paste
  • 120 ml
    lemon juice
  • 3/4 tsp
    salt
  1. Crush garlic and mash together with salt and cayenne to form a paste
  2. Whisk in the tahini with a fork
  3. Thin the mixture with lemon juice
  4. Can be kept in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. Can also be frozen
