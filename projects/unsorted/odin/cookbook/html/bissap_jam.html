Bissap (Hibiscus) Jam

  • 500 ml
    bissap leaves
  • 1/4 k
    sugar
  • 1 tsp
    vanilla
  1. Soak leaves in water for 4-8 hr
  2. Boil to a paste (30-45 min)
  3. Add sugar
  4. Reboil if necessary
  5. Jar
  6. Place jars in a pot of water, bring to a boil and boil for 30min to seal and sterelize
