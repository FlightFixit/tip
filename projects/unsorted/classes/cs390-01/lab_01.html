Lab #1: Basic Robot Construction

In Class: 14:35 Thrs., 31 January 2008

All work should be done on your own and any collaboration or additional resources should be indicated on your assignment.

The previous homework assignment involved the simulation of the Lego Tribot. In this lab we will construct the actual robot from a mindstorms kit.

The instructions for constructing the Tribot are included with the Mindstorms package.

The estimated construction time for this robot is half an hour. The NXT system used in constructing the robot has a control program for Robotics Studio installed when you get it. Pick one of your team's submissions for homework #1 and use it to control your Tribot. Instructions can be found at:
