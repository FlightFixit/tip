About the Chinese Character List
Some Sources

The list of characters on the Basic Chinese Characters page were compiled by me from primarily two sources, both of which are little travel books. Here are the references:

  • Sussman, Julie Mazel, I Can READ That! A Traveler's Introduction to Chinese Characters, China Books & Periodicals, Inc., San Francisco, 1996
  • Sally Davies, ed., et al., Mandarin Chinese, A Rough Guide Phrasebook, Rough Guides Ltd., London, 1997
Of course, I learned a lot of these words from someone special. *wink, wink*
About the Tester

The Tester found on the Chinese Character/Pinyin Test page uses all the characters found on the Chinese Character List mentioned above.
